Our Exciting Rafting Trips

Family Adventure

Perfect for beginners and families, this gentle ride takes you through scenic stretches of the river with mild rapids and plenty of opportunities to spot wildlife. Our experienced guides ensure a safe and fun experience for all ages. Duration: 3 hours. Includes snacks and safety gear.

Thrill Seeker Rapids

For those craving excitement, our Thrill Seeker Rapids trip offers heart-pounding action through Class III and IV rapids. Navigate challenging waters and enjoy the adrenaline rush with our expert team. Suitable for ages 14 and up. Duration: 4 hours. Includes lunch and all equipment.

Scenic Sunset Float

Unwind with a peaceful evening float as the sun sets over the river. This relaxing trip is ideal for couples and photographers, offering breathtaking views and a tranquil atmosphere. Duration: 2 hours. Includes refreshments and a complimentary photo package.
